  • Mango Chia Pudding

    • Ingredients:

    • 1/2 Mango
    • 1 cup of milk (of your choice)
    • 2 tablespoons of Greek Yogurt
    • 1/4 cup of Chia Seeds
    • 1 tablespoon of Maple syrup or Honey (Optional)

    1. Add the mango, milk and Greek Yogurt to a blender and blend until everything is fully combined

    2. Pour the mix into a cup and add the chia seeds and Maple syrup

    3. With a spoon mix everything

    4. Put it on the fridge overnight

    5. (Optional) The next morning add mango pieces and greek Yogurt on top

    Enjoy!!

  • Egg Quesadilla

    • Ingredients:

    • 2 Whole Eggs
    • 1 tortilla
    • Cheese
    • Vegetables (Tomato, Spinach)
    • 1 tablespoon of Corn (Optional)

    1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well combined.

    2. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.

    3. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook them, stirring gently

    4. Add corn if using

    5. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook them, stirring gently

    6. Place the tortilla on top of the eggs in the pan

    7. Sprinkle cheese over everything

    8. Cook for another 1–2 minutes until the cheese melts and the tortilla is slightly golden

    9. Take out of the stove, and add the vegetables

    10. Carefully fold the tortilla in half to form a quesadilla

    11. Enjoy!!

  • Colombian Migao (A traditional recipe from my country, hope you like it :)

    • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup of Milk (of your choice)
    • 3 or 4 tablespoons of Cocoa Powder
    • 1 or 2 tablespoons of sugar
    • 1 piece of day-old bread (like a baguette or any crusty bread)
    • Fresh white cheese (like mozzarella or a mild farmer’s cheese)

    1. Heat the milk in a pot over medium heat

    2. Add the cocoa powder and sugar, stirring well until everything is dissolved

    3. Let it warm up, but do not let it boil

    4. Serve it warm in a cup

    5. Add the pieces of bread into the hot chocolate

    6. Let the bread soak and soften for a few minutes

    7. Add small pieces of fresh white cheese

    8. Let it sit until the cheese softens slightly

    9. enjoy this cozy treat!!

  • waffles

    • Ingredients:

    • 2 cups of Flour
    • 1 teaspoon of Salt
    • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
    • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
    • 2 Eggs
    • 1 1/3 cups of warm milk
    • 1/3 cup of melted Butter
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    1. Preheat your waffle maker or pan if yu don't have one at home

    2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar

    3. In another bowl, whisk the eggs, then add the milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract

    4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until combined

    5. Pour the batter into the waffle maker or pan

    6. Cook until golden and crispy

    7. Serve with your favorite toppings (fruits, syrup, yogurt, etc.)

    Enjoy!!

  • Blueberry muffins

    • Ingredients:

    • 1/2 cup of softened Butter
    • 1 1/4 cups of white Sugar
    • 2 Eggs
    • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • 2 cups of flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt
    • 2 teaspoons of Baking Powder
    • 1/2 cup of Milk
    • 2 cups of Blueberries

    1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F)

    2. In a bowl Wisk all the dry ingredients

    3. In a different bowl, wisk all the wet ingredients until fully combined

    4. Now pour in the wet mixture with the dry one and mix until just combine

    5. Stir through the blueberries

    6. Divide the batter between paper cases or a muffin mold

    7. Bake for 5 minutes, then turn oven down to 180°C (350°)

    8. Bake for a further 15 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the muffin comes out clean

    9. Take them out of the oven and let them cool until they are warm or fully cooled (Warm is super yummy)

    Enjoy!!
